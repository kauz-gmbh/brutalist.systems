Brutalist Systems

Form follows function.

I want to share.

I am tired of the internet. I am tired of tracking, everything being about money, and the constant barrage of rage-bait brainrot gacha-game popup madness. I yearn for simplicity, sharing meaningful experiences with old and new friends, and actually having fun. In the internet of 2025, I haven't felt joy for a long time.

Besides giving up on the internet as a whole, all I can really do is build. Small, quiet tools that do one thing well. They won't scream for attention, harvest data, and, most importantly, they won't pretend to be more than they are.

I don't know if it's naïve. But maybe, in the internet of 2025, it's the only way left to carve out a corner of the web that feels human again.
